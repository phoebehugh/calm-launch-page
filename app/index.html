Calm have built the technology to detect when you're experiencing stress through analysing heart rate, perspiration, blood pressure and movement.

If we recognise that you're reaching a stress level, we send an alert and ask you to breathe with us.

Calm uses a breathing exercise that is scientifically proven to reduce stress levels, and we keep breathing with you until you're heart rate returns to a safe healthy state.
