Calm is the first real-time stress detection app on the Apple Watch, helping you to manage your stress level and stay healthy under pressure.

Calm have built the technology to detect when you're experiencing stress through analysing heart rate, perspiration, blood pressure and movement.

If we recognise that you're reaching a stress level, we send an alert and ask you to engage in a breathing exercise.

Calm uses a breathing exercise that is scientifically proven to reduce stress levels, and we keep breathing with you until your heart rate returns to a safe and healthy state.

Initially designed to help you fall asleep in under one minute, the "4-7-8 Breathing Exercise“ is also effective in rapidly reducing your heart-rate.

The movement of the Calm Droplet is designed to focus your mind whilst you concentrate on your breathing. It also gives you live bio-feedback on your decreasing heart rate.

Calm runs seamlessly in the background of your Apple Watch and only engages with you when it has to, caring for your health as you get on with your life.
